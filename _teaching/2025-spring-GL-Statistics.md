Guest lecture in the course Robot Design and Experimentation taught by Dr. Victoria A. Webster-Wood. In this lecture, I introduced students to the development of experiments using their robotic systems and how to understand, develop, apply, and interpret frequentist statistical tools to analyze their experiments.
